# The Freedom Ready Business™ Framework

**Built on the CEPA foundation** (Certified Exit Planning Advisor — Value Acceleration
Methodology, the Three Readinesses, and the Four C's of intangible capital),
integrated with Kurt Baker's **CFP®** (personal financial independence) and **AIF®**
(fiduciary investment discipline).

Two layers: the public **outcome model** (R.E.A.D.Y.) and the internal **delivery
process** (APEX).

---

## R.E.A.D.Y. Framework™ — the 5 pillars (the "what")

The acronym reinforces the brand ("Freedom **Ready** Business") and maps to the five
outcomes of a business that gives you options, not obligations.

| Letter | Pillar | Meaning | CEPA / CFP / AIF tie-in |
|---|---|---|---|
| **R** | Revenue Resilience | Predictable, recurring income that doesn't depend on the owner. | Business readiness |
| **E** | Enterprise Value | Sellable, not just profitable — the Four C's: human, customer, structural, social capital. | CEPA value acceleration |
| **A** | Autonomy | Runs and grows without the owner — systems, team, leadership. | Business readiness |
| **D** | De-Risked & Diversified | The 5 D's covered (death, disability, divorce, distress, disagreement) + tax, liquidity, investing. | CFP / AIF |
| **Y** | Your Exit, Your Terms | Exit-ready: scale, sell, recapitalize, or step back — on your timeline. | CEPA Master Plan |

## APEX Method™ — the 4-step process (the "how")

| Step | CEPA gate | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| **A — Assess** | Discover | Score enterprise value, owner-dependency, readiness → the Freedom Score™ baseline. |
| **P — Protect** | (De-risk) | Shore up financial infrastructure, tax, liquidity, and the 5 D's. |
| **E — Elevate** | Prepare | Accelerate the Four C's of capital, recurring revenue, owner-free systems. |
| **X — eXit-Ready** | Decide | Build the Master Plan that turns options into freedom. |
